# Resources: Further Reading & Tools for BioAI Product Managers

## Key Guidelines & Standards
- **FDA AI/ML-Based SaMD Action Plan** (2021) and subsequent PCCP guidance (fda.gov)
- **FDA Clinical Decision Support Software Guidance** — non-device CDS criteria (21st Century Cures Act)
- **EU AI Act** — high-risk AI system requirements applicable to medical AI (europa.eu)
- **ICH E6(R2)/(R3)** — Good Clinical Practice, relevant for clinical AI validation studies
- **IMDRF AI/ML SaMD guidance** — international harmonization efforts

## Landmark Papers & Resources
- Jumper et al. (2021) — "Highly accurate protein structure prediction with AlphaFold" (Nature)
- Topol (2019) — "High-performance medicine: the convergence of human and artificial intelligence" (Nature Medicine)
- Obermeyer et al. (2019) — "Dissecting racial bias in an algorithm used to manage the health of populations" (Science) — landmark bias case study
- FDA's published list of AI/ML-enabled medical devices (fda.gov) — useful for competitive/regulatory landscape awareness

## Books
- **"Inspired: How to Create Tech Products Customers Love"** (Marty Cagan) — general PM foundations, applicable with domain adaptation
- **"The Innovator's Prescription"** (Christensen et al.) — disruptive innovation framework applied to healthcare
- **"Deep Medicine"** (Eric Topol) — AI in medicine landscape overview

## Organizations & Communities
- **DIA (Drug Information Association)** — cross-industry pharma/biotech conferences and publications
- **HIMSS** — health IT industry conference and resources
- **MLSB (Machine Learning for Structural Biology)** workshop community — NeurIPS-affiliated
- **Product-Led Alliance / Mind the Product** — general PM community resources adaptable to BioAI context

## Regulatory Resources
- FDA Digital Health Center of Excellence (fda.gov)
- EMA guidance on AI in medicines regulation (ema.europa.eu)
- CDISC standards documentation (cdisc.org) — relevant for clinical AI validation data structuring

## Landscape/Market Awareness
- Company S-1 filings and public investor materials from major BioAI companies (Recursion, Insitro, Isomorphic Labs, Tempus, etc.) — useful for business model and GTM pattern analysis
- Public benchmark leaderboards (e.g., CASP for structure prediction, Therapeutics Data Commons) — useful for technical landscape awareness
